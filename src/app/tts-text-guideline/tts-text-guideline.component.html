TTS Text Guidelines

Rule How to Use Example
Sentence Breaks Use `.`, `!`, `?` for natural pauses The quick brown fox jumps. It lands gracefully!
Commas Use `,` for short, flowing pauses I tried, I failed, I learned.
Explicit Pauses [pause] for short, [longpause] for long pauses Wait for it[pause]... here it comes.[longpause]
Emphasis [em:word] to stress important words Remember to [em:review] daily.
Paragraph Breaks Split content into 1–3 sentences per chunk Avoid long unbroken paragraphs for better pacing.
Numbers & Acronyms Spell out numbers or acronyms for clarity AI → A.I., 2025 → twenty twenty-five
